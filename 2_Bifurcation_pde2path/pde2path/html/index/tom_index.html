tom index

  • arc2tom: convert arc-data-struc to tom (e.g., for meshref in t)
  • mtom: modification of TOM to deal with mass matrix M
  • tom2arc: init p.hopf.tau and p.hopf.y0d from p.hopf.ysec after nat.-cont
  • tomassem: assemble rhs and jac for hopf (arclength), extracted from TOM
  • tomassemF: assemble rhs for hopf (arclength), extracted from TOM
  • tomassempbc: assemble rhs and jac for hopf with pBC, pad y accordingly
  • tomget: extract options for TOM, by Francesca Mazzia
  • tominit: form initial guess for TOM, by Francesca Mazzia
  • tomset: from TOM
  • tomsol: solves hopf at fixed lam using tom with aux-vars for pBC and T
  • v2tom: convert soln vector to matrix for tom
